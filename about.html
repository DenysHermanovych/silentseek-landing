About SilentSeek

SilentSeek LLC is an early-stage technology startup building an industrial acoustic monitoring platform designed to protect critical infrastructure and ensure commercial airspace safety using distributed IoT sensor networks and AWS cloud AI.

🎯

Mission

Democratize high-precision acoustic monitoring technology to make critical infrastructure and commercial environments safer.

👁️

Vision

A world where every industrial facility and commercial site has real-time awareness of its acoustic environment, powered by AI.

⚡

Approach

Edge-first sensing with ESP32 microcontrollers, secured by AWS IoT Core, processed by ML on EC2, stored in S3 — all in under 50ms.

Activities & Services

Hardware & Firmware Engineering

We design and program custom IoT sensor nodes based on ESP32 microcontrollers. These edge devices capture high-fidelity acoustic data and perform initial signal processing (FFT) locally to reduce latency and bandwidth.

AWS Cloud Architecture

Our core service is providing a robust cloud backend. We utilize AWS IoT Core for secure MQTT communication, Amazon EC2 for advanced machine learning analytics, and Amazon S3 for long-term acoustic data storage.

Data Visualization & Analytics

We deliver a centralized web dashboard that translates complex audio signatures into actionable spectrograms and real-time anomaly alerts for enterprise clients.

System Integration Consulting

We assist industrial and commercial partners in deploying our sensor networks into their existing infrastructure without requiring physical modifications to their machinery.
